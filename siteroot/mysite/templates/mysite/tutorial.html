Simple 5 step guide to using Meshwell
Step 1: Login/Sign Up
Simply Sign up and Login with your account on the top right hand corner to get started!


Step 2: Connect
Click 'Dashboard' on the navigation bar to set up your initial settings!
  • Click on the "Connect" button, to connect your in game account to Meshwell.


  • Click on the 'Add new Availability' button, to add your availabilities and timings.


  • Click on the 'Preferences' button, to rank and choose what the most important qualities you're looking for in a team-mate are.


  • Click on the "Connect" button under the personal profile on the left hand side of the page to connect your discord and recieve messages.


Step 3: Matchmake
Click on the Matchmake buttons to find gamers based on your preferences!
  • 'Smart Matcmake' uses our algorithm to find you a team compatible players.


  • 'Manual Matchmake' gives you a list of potential Players that you can select from.




Step 4: Communicate
Get on Discord and start communicating with your uniqely selected team-mate!
  • Our discord bot will create a room with all your matched team-mates and message you!


Step 5: Review
Review your matches once completed!
