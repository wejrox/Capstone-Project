About Us

Meshwell is a collaboration project as part of RMIT's "Programming Project 1 - COSC2408" course.
The project strives to provide a smart matchmaking service to gamers who can't find other gamers to play with by using an algorithm that matches certain metrics to ensure that all matched players are on the same "level". Metrics used in Meshwell's algorithm include confirmed MMR levels from the game itself, availability time of players, region, if want to play competitive playlists, and also uses a commendation system to help filter out positive and negative players.
