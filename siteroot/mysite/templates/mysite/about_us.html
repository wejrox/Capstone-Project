About Us
Meshwell is a smart matchmaking service that aims to connect gamers together by using an algorithm that matches based on a variety of factors to ensure that matched players are on the same skill level. Factors included are confirmed MMR levels from the game itself, availability time of players, regions of players, competitive matching, and also uses a commendation system to help filter out positive players from negative ones.
